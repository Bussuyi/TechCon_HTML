Our History

TechCon began over a decade ago as a small gathering of innovators who wanted to share their passion for technology. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated conferences in the tech industry, attracting thousands of attendees from around the globe.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Jane Smith

Dr. Jane Smith is a pioneer in artificial intelligence, leading research that has shaped the way we interact with AI in everyday life. At TechCon 2020, she shared her insights on ethical AI.

Michael Johnson

Michael Johnson is a renowned entrepreneur and founder of several successful startups. His TechCon 2021 keynote focused on building resilient companies in a rapidly changing world.

Sophia Lee

Sophia Lee is a cybersecurity expert who has advised governments and corporations. At TechCon 2022, she spoke about safeguarding digital assets in the modern era.
